How it works ?

We are displaying here all tricks that needs to be validated. You can tell if they are accurate or not, meaning the time range is well specified, the sport, the trick and the name of the performer are corrects. You have three choices:

  • - Skip: if your not sure, or you don't know please skip
  • - Accurate: if you are sure that everything is valid, click on accurate.
  • - Not accurate: if some of the info are not valid, such as the performer or the category, click on not accurate.

If you want, you can also propose a better trick, for example if the landing is cutted too soon or the name of the trick is not accurate. To do so just click on "Propose a better one"
